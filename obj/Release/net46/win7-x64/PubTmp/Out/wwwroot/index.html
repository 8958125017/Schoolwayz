Making Schools Smart & Secure

Introducing the SchoolWayz Suite
next step in direction of being smart & secure

SchoolWayz suite includes multiple applications utilizing cutting edge technology
including Biometric, GPS, IOT, Cloud and SmartPhone

SmartId

Utilizes Biometric (Fingerprint)/RFID to automate manual process of student/staff attendance taking. Notifications are sent to parents in real-time to inform about students. Our custom built SchoolSmart® IOT Devices are used to process & store data in cloud and provide valuable analytics to school administrator.

SmartTrack

Utilizes real-time information via GPS tracking built in our custom SchoolSmart® IOT devices. Parent’s are able to review current location of bus and receive notifications. Provide information about driving speed , routes, stops to administrator. Support is also available for surveillance via external Camera module.

SmartConnect

Uses cloud based messaging system to connect students, teachers , parents and school administration. Provides ability to send messages internal to teachers/students on SchoolSmart® devices, and external to parents on mobile app. Acknowledgments and feedback can be reviewed by administrator via Web Portal.

SmartPay

Makes secure digital payments/prepayments convenient for parents and staff. Parent’s enjoy the security & flexibility via paying with mobile or web app. SmartId can be integrated with the use of SchoolSmart® IOT devices for cashless payments in school and eliminate's the need to carry cash. Parents are notified for all the payment activities.
